Master Bedroom

The Master Suite is luxuriously furnished to the highest level by local designer talent and products to give you a truly unique taste of the west. The views overlook the pristine Galway Bay and the river Corrib. With a master bedroom, cozy living room and own private bathroom with full size bath and separate walk-in shower, you’ll have all the comfort of a high class apartment, with round the clock service. The walnut wooden floors have underfloor heating throughout, and the private balcony is heated and covered so it can be enjoyed in any weather. Breakfast is available in the restaurant or as room service on request so you can take it easy and make the most of your stay. On arrival, please enjoy the complimentary seasonal refreshments from local artisans.

Zen pack Room

Every detail of our zen room has been designed with a philosophy of relaxation on the deepest levels to help you find your centre and find your way back to your self. The room can be arranged to house one or two guests, is fully sound proofed to allow you to escape the hustle and bustle, and with fully customisable lighting, a range of locally sourced aromatherapy on request, and optional pre-booked massage therapy, you’ll feel like you have your own private Spa. The living space has an eastern style design with bamboo floors and plenty of space to lounge, meditate or even practice your morning yoga. To complete the experience, the stone bathroom includes whirlpool deep soaking tubs and separate walk-in tropical rain shower.

Cozy Suite

The west coast of Ireland is wild and beautiful, but after a long day exploring its rugged coastlines and mountain trails, there’s nothing better than getting cozy by the fire with a cup of tea in the evening. Our ground floor suite offers a fireside lounge and enchanting bedroom, both decked out with authentic Aran knitted woollen throws for a true taste of Ireland. Patio doors lead out to a private shomera or garden room with its own garden space and an outdoor hot tub for the ultimate getaway. The bathroom also has garden views and a candle lit feel lighting set up that will make you feel right at home.

Studio Suite

Our studio suite offers a spacious living room with mezzanine double bed space, in a sleek, modern style. The living room can double as a comfortable office or meeting space, or you can extend the two fold out couches for additional bed space. Perfect for a business trip or short stay, enjoy private access to a personal garage, fully stocked mini bar and free safe. For that extra special touch, the studio suite can be pre decorated in a theme of your choice for small parties and celebrations.
