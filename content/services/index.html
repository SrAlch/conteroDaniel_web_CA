What can we do for you?

Taxi Service

We want to offer the most relaxing and confortable experience for you, our staff would gladly help you to request in advance a Taxi from Galway Airport (GWY) at 8km / 20min or Shannon Airport (SNN) at 80km / 60min to make you stay worryless. If you preffer bus to arrive to olur facilities there are shuttle services from both airports that we will prebook for you if requested. The nearest bus stop is 45 from our doors!

Bicycles for Rent

If you are looking for some ecological adventure on wheels or hopping to get around the city to explore it without the limitations that a motor vehicle bring, we put ar your service our city and off road bicycle fleet. Availability will vary depending on seasson and height. Don't forget to add your bicycles when you are booking your room. If you have already a reservation without bicycles and changed your mind, don't hesitate and contact us, we will do our best to facilitate you our services.

Gastronomic Range

Our restaurant prides itself on embracing the very best of local cuisine. From traditional Irish roasts and stews, the freshest Atlantic sea food to our hearty range of farm fresh vegetarian and vegan dishes, we cater to any pallet. Please call ahead with special dietary requirements so we can ensure our staff are fully prepared to meet your needs.

Traditional Music

Traditional Irish music is vibrant and widespread in the west, and our public lounge is proud to host regular sessions Friday to Sunday, 8 till late. Come along early for private practice sessions and the chance to try your hand at unique instruments, some of which are rarely ever found outside of Ireland. The players are always welcoming and eager to share this wonderful art with locals ad visitors alike.

Beautiful Beaches

Enjoy a stroll along the Salthill promenade or take a short trip to one of the nearby beaches along the coast of Galway. The west coast of Ireland hosts some of the most beautiful swimming beaches in the world, and you can’t miss the chance of a sunset over the Atlantic.

Horseback Riding

With so much to see and do around Galway, it can be good to get off your feet and take a trek along the beaches or into the mountain trails. Let us guide you to the best riding centres to suit your needs, or organise the event for you. Horse treks can cater to any level, so whether you want to break new ground or you’re an avid rider already, we can find the trail that’s right for you.

Golf Courses

The Emerald Isle is famous for its greenery, and it’s no wonder we host a wide array of golf clubs. Our staff can advise on the local courses or arrange a tee time or party for you. Weather permitting, irelands climate allows for golfing all year round.

Fishing

Galway city lies at the mouth of the river Corrib as it flows into the Atlantic Ocean. This gives us the perfect position for both river and sea fishing. License a boat to head out for some deep sea fishing for species such as shark, ray and cod, or head inland for a more relaxed experience fishing in the rivers and lakes. For an extra special experience, book a trip to Loughbey Corrib to go pike fishing.

Water sports

For the adventurous soul, the true charm of the west coast are its numerous waterways. From canoeing and Kayaking around the bay, to some of the best surfing in the world. Take a kayak out for a sea tour for a unique perspective on the rugged Galway coastline.
