The Capital of the West

Come stay with us in the heart of Galway city, the historical home of the King of Connacht, and a halfway house of the Wild Atlantic Way.

Soak up the history of our beautiful and lively streets, full of colourful cafes and bars, live traditional music and the best of Atlantic sea food. Take advantage of the small town shops in the city of arts, unwind as you explore the museums and sights of the city, or enjoy the nightlife. Galway has something for every visitor. We are also a festival town and would delight in advising you on any arts and film events coinciding with your stay.

If you need a break from the hustle and bustle, take an adventure beyond the city walls to explore the rugged west coast of Ireland. The city is a stones throw from some of the most varied and scenic landscapes you can find, from long stretches of beach, to the unique landscape of the Burren region. However you choose to spend your stay, we'll be here to make it memorable.
